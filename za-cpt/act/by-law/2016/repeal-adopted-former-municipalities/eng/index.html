Repeal of By-laws Adopted by Former Municipalities By-law, 2016

  • Published in Western Cape Provincial Gazette 7731 on 10 February 2017
  • Assented to on 7 December 2016
  • Commenced in full on 10 February 2017
  • [This is the version of this document from 10 February 2017 and includes any amendments published up to 28 July 2023.]
To provide for the repeal of redundant by-laws adopted by former municipalities in the jurisdiction of the City of Cape Town; and to provide for matters incidental thereto.BE IT ENACTED by the Council of the City of Cape Town, as follows:

1. Repeal of By-laws

The by-laws listed in the Schedule, which were adopted by former municipalities, the areas of which were incorporated in the City of Cape Town, are hereby repealed, insofar as they are applicable in the area of jurisdiction of the City of Cape Town.

2. Short title

Short title This By-law is called the City of Cape Town: By-Law Relating to the Repeal of By-Laws Adopted by Former Municipalities, 2016.
